Learning Package 3

EoL Clinical Management
& Medication Safety

Practical guidance for managing diabetes in the final stages of life — where clinical goals shift from prevention to comfort, safety, and the appropriate rationalisation of therapy.

⏱ ~25 minutes
📋 10 slides
🎯 2 interactive activities
Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module you will be able to:

  • 1
    Apply appropriate glycaemic targets for patients in the dying phase.
  • 2
    Rationalise non-insulin drug therapies to minimise adverse effects at end of life.
  • 3
    Identify and manage diabetic emergencies — hypoglycaemia and hyperglycaemia — in a palliative setting.
