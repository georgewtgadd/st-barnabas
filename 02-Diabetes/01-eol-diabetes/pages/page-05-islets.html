Normal Islet Function

Beta cell mass is sufficient to produce adequate insulin. Blood glucose regulation is intact.

Impaired Insulin Secretion

Beta cells are declining. Insulin production is insufficient — pre-diabetic state or early diabetes is likely.

🔴
Severe Insulin Deficiency

Critical loss of beta cell mass. Absolute insulin deficiency — characteristic of late Type 1 or advanced pancreatic disease. Without exogenous insulin, DKA is inevitable.
