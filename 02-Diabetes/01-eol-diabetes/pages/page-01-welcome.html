Clinical Training Module

End of Life
Diabetes Management

This package provides essential training for managing diabetes in palliative and end-of-life care — where the clinical goals shift fundamentally from long-term prevention to immediate comfort and dignity.

Learning Outcomes
  • 1
    Explain the homeostatic cycle of insulin and glucagon in maintaining blood glucose levels.
  • 2
    Differentiate between the five types of diabetes and their distinct pathophysiologies.
  • 3
    Identify normal blood glucose ranges for non-diabetics versus those with Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes.
