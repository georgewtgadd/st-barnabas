Why this matters in EoL care

Glucose is the body's primary fuel. When diabetes is poorly controlled at end of life, cells are starved of energy despite rising blood sugar. Understanding this equation helps explain why even small doses of insulin may be needed to maintain cellular function — and why sudden withdrawal can cause acute distress.

✅ Equation Complete!
C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + ATP

Glucose + Oxygen → Carbon Dioxide + Water + Energy
Every cell in the body runs on this reaction. Without sufficient insulin to allow glucose uptake, even a well-oxygenated cell cannot produce energy — and the patient deteriorates. In EoL care, our goal is to maintain just enough glucose supply for comfort, not perfection.
