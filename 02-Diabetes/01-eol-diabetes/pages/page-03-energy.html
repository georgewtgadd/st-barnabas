Why this matters in EoL care

Glucose is the body's primary fuel. When diabetes is poorly controlled at end of life, cells are starved of energy despite rising blood sugar. Understanding this equation helps explain why even small doses of insulin may be needed to maintain cellular function — and why sudden withdrawal can cause acute distress.
