EoL Relevance

At end of life, reduced oral intake drives patients toward the low-glucose pathway. Simultaneously, corticosteroids push the high-glucose pathway — a clinical tug-of-war. Insulin doses must be proactively reduced as intake falls to prevent hypoglycaemia, and steroid-induced hyperglycaemia must be monitored.
