EoL Relevance

At end of life, reduced oral intake shifts patients toward the low-glucose pathway. Insulin doses should be adjusted downward to prevent hypoglycaemia. Glucocorticoid use simultaneously pushes the high-glucose pathway — creating a clinical tug-of-war requiring careful balance.
