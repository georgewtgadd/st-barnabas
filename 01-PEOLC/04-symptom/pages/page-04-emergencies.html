Palliative Emergencies

A palliative emergency is a sudden change that, if not managed promptly, will cause extreme distress or rapid irreversible deterioration. Knowing the difference between expected decline and a clinical emergency requiring urgent action is a fundamental skill.

Not every change in a palliative patient's condition is an emergency. Knowing the difference between expected deterioration and a clinical emergency requiring urgent action can mean the difference between comfort and catastrophe.
