Helen's Clinical Notes
Personal

Full name: Helen Marsh. Date of birth: 14 March 1951 (age 75). Lives at home with husband Simon Marsh (68) and granddaughter Sophie Marsh (14). Faith: Christian — faith is central to her coping.

Clinical

Stage 4 ovarian cancer with pulmonary metastases. Oxygen therapy 2L/min in place. Prognosis: weeks to months. DNR in place. Medication review due this visit.

What Matters to Helen

Helen values her dignity and privacy deeply. She does not want to be seen as a burden. She wants clinical discussions to take place sensitively, in her presence only. She prays regularly and finds faith her main source of peace.

Care Preferences

Helen has expressed a strong wish to remain at home for as long as possible. She has not formally stated a preferred place of death but has expressed she does not want to die in hospital. She would consider the hospice if home care is no longer manageable.

Family Context

Simon is primary carer — working night shifts while managing Helen's care, showing signs of carer strain. Sophie (14) has not been told the prognosis and is withdrawing — consider referral to youth bereavement services. Financial pressures noted (heating bills).

Documents in Place

DNR / DNACPR confirmed. No LPA on file yet — to be discussed. No formal ACP documented. Medication list attached to care record. ReSPECT form not yet completed.
