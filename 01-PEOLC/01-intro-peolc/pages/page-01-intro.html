Palliative Care Fundamentals · Module 1

What is End of Life
Palliative Care?

This module explores what palliative and end of life care really means — clearing up common myths, examining clinical guidance from the GMC and NICE, and helping you feel confident using this language with patients and families.

📚

How to use this module: Work through each section in order. Some pages include activities — complete them to unlock the next step. Your reflection and quiz score will be saved to your personal learning record.

By the end of this module
You will be able to…
1
Define palliative care and end of life care using recognised clinical definitions
2
Describe the five phases of end of life care and the six layers of palliative support
3
Apply the GMC & NICE 12-month definition to identify patients approaching end of life
4
Challenge common myths and misconceptions about palliative care in your practice
5
Reflect on the impact of language in end of life conversations
