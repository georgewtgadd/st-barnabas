Assessing Capacity

The Two-Stage Test for assessing mental capacity under the MCA (2005). Drag the four elements of Stage 2 into the correct order of assessment.

Stage 1 — The Diagnostic Threshold

Is there an impairment of, or disturbance in the functioning of, the mind or brain? This includes conditions such as dementia, acquired brain injury, severe mental illness, or intoxication. If Stage 1 is not met, the person is presumed to have capacity and the assessment stops here.
