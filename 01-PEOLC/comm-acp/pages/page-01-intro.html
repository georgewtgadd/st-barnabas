Palliative Care Fundamentals · Module 5

Communication &
Advance Care Planning

This module explores the legal frameworks and communication skills required to support person-centred care at the end of life. You will learn how to ensure a patient's wishes are documented, legally protected, and sensitively communicated.

📚

How to use this module: Work through each section in order. Some pages include activities — complete them to unlock the next step. Your activity results and quiz score will be saved to your personal learning record.

By the end of this module
You will be able to…
1
Critique communication styles using Mehrabian's Rule to improve sensitive conversations
2
Execute a mental capacity assessment in line with the Mental Capacity Act (2005)
3
Distinguish between an ADRT and a ReSPECT form and their legal statuses
4
Apply a Best Interests decision-making process in a challenging clinical scenario
5
Formulate clear clinical recommendations using ACP tools for a specific case
