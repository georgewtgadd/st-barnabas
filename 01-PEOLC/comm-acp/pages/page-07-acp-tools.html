The ReSPECT process is a person-centred conversation that creates a shared understanding between patient, family and clinical team about goals and priorities for future emergency care. While not legally binding, a well-completed ReSPECT form guides emergency clinicians when time is short. It should reflect the patient's wishes and clinical realities — completed with the patient, not done to them.
